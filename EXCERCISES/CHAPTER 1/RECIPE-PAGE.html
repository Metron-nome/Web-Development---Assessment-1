Berried Delight

"Berried" treasure is in every bite of these creamy vanilla no-bake squares. Take 'em to a party and watch these gems disappear.


INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cups Honey Maid速 Graham Cracker Crumbs

  • 1/4 cup sugar

  • 6 Tbsp. butter or margarine

  • 1 package Philadelphia速 Cream Cheese

  • 2 Tbsp. cold milk

  • 1 tub Cool Whip速 Whipped Topping

  • 4 cups strawberries

  • 3 1/2 cups cold milk

  • 2 package Jell-O速 Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding

  • Additional sliced fresh strawberries

STEPS

  1. Mix crumbs, sugar, and butter in 13- x 9-inch pan; press onto bottom of pan. Refrigerate 15 minutes.

  2. Beat cream cheese and 2 tablespoons milk in large bowl with wire whisk until well blended. Gently stir in half of the whipped topping. Spread onto crust; top with strawberries. Pour 3 1/2 cups milk into large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes. Beat 2 minutes. Pour over strawberries.

  3. Refrigerate 4 hours or until set. Spread with remaining whipped topping just before serving. Store leftovers in refrigerator.

  4. For an extra special touch, garnish with additional sliced fresh strawberries just before serving.
